Government Schemes

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

A central sector scheme to provide income support to all landholding farmers' families in the country.

  • ₹6000 per year income support
  • Paid in three installments of ₹2000
  • Direct benefit transfer to farmers
  • Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers owning up to 2 hectares of cultivable land
  • Exclusions: Institutional landholders, higher-income professionals, and taxpayers
  • Implemented by the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers' Welfare

Ayushman Bharat

World's largest health insurance scheme aimed at providing coverage of ₹5 lakh per family per year.

  • Cashless treatment
  • Coverage for secondary and tertiary care
  • Portable across the country
  • Covers nearly 10 crore vulnerable families (approx. 50 crore beneficiaries)
  • Implemented under Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)
  • Eligibility: Based on SECC (Socio-Economic Caste Census) data
  • Over 24,000 hospitals empaneled across India

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana

Housing scheme to provide affordable housing to the urban and rural poor.

  • Interest subsidy on home loans
  • Direct financial assistance
  • Both urban and rural coverage
  • Beneficiaries: Economically Weaker Sections (EWS), Low Income Groups (LIG), Middle-Income Groups (MIG)
  • Implemented by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs
  • Under PMAY-Rural, houses with basic amenities are provided

Skill India Mission

Initiative to train over 40 crore people in different skills by 2022.

  • Free skill training
  • Industry partnerships
  • Certification programs
  • Key programs: Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), National Skill Development Mission
  • Aims to make youth employable in various industries
  • Training provided in over 40 sectors

Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana

Scheme to provide LPG connections to women from BPL households.

  • Free LPG connection
  • Financial support for first refill
  • Easy loan facility
  • Eligibility: Women from BPL (Below Poverty Line) families
  • Implemented by the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas
  • Aim: Reduce health hazards from traditional cooking fuels
  • More than 9 crore LPG connections distributed since launch

MNREGA

Provides 100 days of guaranteed wage employment to rural households.

  • Employment for unskilled manual labor
  • Focus on rural development projects
  • Implemented by the Ministry of Rural Development
  • Wages directly transferred to bank accounts
  • Enhances livelihood security
  • Supports women and marginalized communities

Stand Up India

Provides loans to SC/ST and women entrepreneurs.

  • Loans from ₹10 lakh to ₹1 crore
  • Focus on Greenfield enterprises
  • Implemented by SIDBI and Banks
  • Encourages entrepreneurship
  • Reduces financial barriers
  • Supports job creation

Startup India

Encourages startups by providing benefits and funding support.

  • Tax exemptions for startups
  • Fund of Funds (FoF) scheme
  • Easy compliance and self-certification
  • Boosts innovation
  • Creates job opportunities
  • Encourages entrepreneurship

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana

Aims at financial inclusion by providing banking services to all.

  • Zero balance savings accounts
  • Free RuPay debit card
  • Overdraft facility
  • Encourages digital transactions
  • Enables direct benefit transfers
  • Reduces financial exclusion

Digital India

Aims to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy.

  • Promotes digital infrastructure
  • Enhances digital literacy
  • Encourages e-governance
  • Supports digital payment systems
  • Facilitates high-speed internet access
  • Boosts cybersecurity and data protection

Make in India

Encourages manufacturing and investment in India to boost economic growth.

  • Focuses on 25 key sectors
  • Aims to increase domestic production
  • Boosts foreign direct investment (FDI)
  • Provides incentives for manufacturing
  • Enhances ease of doing business
  • Encourages job creation

Jal Jeevan Mission

Aims to provide safe and adequate drinking water through household tap connections.

  • Ensures water supply to rural households
  • Implemented by the Ministry of Jal Shakti
  • Focuses on sustainable water resources
  • Encourages community participation
  • Promotes rainwater harvesting
  • Supports greywater management

Saubhagya Scheme

Aims to achieve universal household electrification in India.

  • Provides free electricity connections to BPL families
  • Implemented by the Ministry of Power
  • Covers both urban and rural areas
  • Promotes energy efficiency
  • Improves quality of life
  • Encourages economic growth

Atal Pension Yojana

Provides pension benefits to unorganized sector workers.

  • Ensures financial security after retirement
  • Subscribers receive fixed monthly pensions
  • Government co-contribution for eligible subscribers
  • Encourages long-term savings
  • Minimum age to enroll is 18 years
  • Managed by the Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority (PFRDA)

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

Aims to make India clean and free from open defecation.

  • Encourages sanitation and hygiene
  • Promotes toilet construction
  • Implemented in rural and urban areas
  • Supports waste management
  • Improves public health
  • Encourages behavioral change

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana

Aims to ensure holistic agricultural development in India.

  • Provides financial support to farmers
  • Focuses on productivity and sustainability
  • Encourages states to invest in agriculture
  • Promotes organic farming
  • Supports technological advancements
  • Boosts farmers' income

PM Matsya Sampada Yojana

Aims to develop the fisheries sector and boost blue economy.

  • Enhances fish production and productivity
  • Provides financial assistance to fishermen
  • Promotes sustainable aquaculture
  • Encourages value addition and export
  • Supports modern infrastructure development
  • Improves livelihood of fishers and fish farmers
